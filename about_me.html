About Me

My name is Diana Wallace and I am an Illustrator. I am currently looking for a job as a Junior Web Developer. I am currently based in Denver, Colorado.

I just finished the Right Skill Web Development program and this website was created through this program. I am also currently finishing up my BFA in Illustration and my portfolio is below. Additionally, the design and coding projects below are through the online front-end web development course that I am currently taking through Thinkful.

I am a dedicated, enthusiastic, and highly motivated illustrator and junior web developer with experience building web sites and creating illustrations. Formally educated in fine art and illustration I also use Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. I have a passion for art, design, and technology and enjoy blending these three mediums along with modern web technologies.

This site was built with cloud9 ide and bootstrap, which is an open source JavaScript framework. The source code is available on Github.
